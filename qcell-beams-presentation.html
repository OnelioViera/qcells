Manufacturing Process

Step-by-step production workflow

1

Custom Forms Fabrication

Specialized hinged formwork manufactured for beam geometry and anchor placement

2

Rebar Cage Assembly

Precision placement of (16) #8 longitudinal bars with #4 stirrups @ 12" o.c.

3

Cage Installation

Rebar assembly positioned in forms with chairs and proper concrete cover

4

Concrete Pour

Type 1/L cement mix, 3% air, 4500 psi @ 28 days per specifications

5

Form Removal

Hinged walls rotate away for easy access and removal from finished beam

6

Beam Extraction

LUA88G lifting anchors enable safe removal and transport to storage
